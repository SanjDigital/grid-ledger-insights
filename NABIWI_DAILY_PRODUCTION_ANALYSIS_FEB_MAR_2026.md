## EXTRACTION METHODOLOGY NOTES

- **Source Format:** Verbatim SMS records in Chichewa with structured field pattern
- **Parsing Logic:** Regex-style extraction of fields in format "Pa [date] Open [X] Close [Y] Ndagayil [Z] Units Amount [MWK] Late [rate]..."
- **Date Interpretation:** "Pa 22.2.26" = 22 February 2026; "Pa 1.3.26" = 1 March 2026
- **Deduplication:** 2 duplicate messages identified and excluded from totals
- **Deduction Items Observed:** 
  - Masefa / Masefas = sieves, screens
  - Magets Kulibe = maize shortage
  - Ma Units Atha = electricity tokens exhausted
  - Ndachotsapo / Ndachotsap = deducted [amount]
  - Yamalipilo Yotsala = maintenance/repair items (context unclear)
- **Accuracy:** All figures recalculated from SMS fields; no rounding beyond source SMS reporting
