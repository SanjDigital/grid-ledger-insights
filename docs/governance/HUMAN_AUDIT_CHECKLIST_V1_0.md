## PURPOSE

This checklist translates the GridLedger Verification Architecture into field-executable procedures. The objective is to produce independent, non-repudiable evidence that:

1. **Physical Infrastructure** exists as described in telemetry (motor, generator, mounting bolts, fuel storage)
2. **Operator Identity** is confirmed and consistent with recorded SMS signatures
3. **Production Continuity** is supported by verifiable operational conditions (no evidence of synthetic reporting)
4. **Telemetry Consent** is explicitly granted and documented

This is **not** a credit assessment. This is a **deterministic witness** to the node's material existence and the operator's consent to participation.
