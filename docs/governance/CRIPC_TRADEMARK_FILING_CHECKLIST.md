## TRADEMARK 1: GRIDLEDGER

**Proposed Mark:** GRIDLEDGER  
**Class:** 42 — Computer Services  
**Scope of Goods/Services:**  
- Software as a Service (SaaS) for energy measurement, verification, and cycle-level cycle reconciliation
- Forensic cycle analysis and hash-based replay verification
- Infrastructure fragility assessment and operator performance separation  
- Measurement and monitoring of agro-processing nodes
- Data validation and deterministic reconstruction services

**Description:**  
GridLedger is a verification infrastructure for energy-gated agro-processing nodes. The service provides cycle-level metered evidence, seal integrity validation, and independent reproducibility of operational outcomes.

**Distinctiveness Notes:**  
- Coined term combining "grid" (electrical) and "ledger" (accounting record)
- Conceptually distinct from generic descriptors
- No known prior registrations in Malawi or ARIPO region

## TRADEMARK 2: THE MEASUREMENT GAP

**Proposed Mark:** THE MEASUREMENT GAP  
**Class:** 41 — Education, Training & Information Services  
**Scope of Goods/Services:**  
- Educational seminars and workshops on energy measurement verification
- Training programs for operators, auditors, and impact investors
- Information services on infrastructure fragility assessment methodology
- Consultancy services on measurement infrastructure design
- Online courses and learning materials on cycle-level reconciliation

**Description:**  
"The Measurement Gap" refers to the institutional inability to separate operator performance from infrastructure-attributable loss in off-grid or constrained-grid energy environments. This mark represents educational and advisory services addressing this gap.

**Distinctiveness Notes:**  
- Conceptual phrase with industry-specific meaning
- Not a common business descriptor in measurement or training contexts
- Supports positioning as thought leadership in agro-processing finance
