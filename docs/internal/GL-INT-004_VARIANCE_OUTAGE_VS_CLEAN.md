# GL-INT-004 — VARIANCE DISTRIBUTION: OUTAGE-AFFECTED vs. CLEAN CYCLES

**Classification:** Internal interpretation only. Not admissible as verification output.
**Source:** EA-004 + 705-cycle sealed dataset

## Statistical Pattern Observation
- Outage-affected cycles (outage message within ±48h of production report) exhibit wider unit variance and higher frequency of partial runs.
- Clean cycles (no proximate outage) show tighter clustering around full expected loads.
- Revenue per cycle distributions differ: outage windows correlate with more "Late" fees and lower net remittances.

## Summary Statistics (Preliminary)
- Outage-affected: higher standard deviation in units ground and amount.
- Clean: more consistent "ma units atha" at expected full loads.

No causal assertion is made. This is a pattern observation for future research design.
