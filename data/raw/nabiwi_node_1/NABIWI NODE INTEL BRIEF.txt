OPERATOR REPORTING PROTOCOL
======================================================================

The operator sends daily SMS production reports in Chichewa using a consistent
structured format. Refer to legacy_system_data_flow_diagram.png in /data.

FORMAT:
  Pa [date] Open [X] Close [Y] Ndagayil [Z] Units Amount [MWK] Late [rate]

FIELD MEANINGS:
  Pa           = "On" (date of report)
  Open         = Meter/tally count at start of shift (20L buckets)
  Close        = Meter/tally count at end of shift
  Ndagayil     = Units milled (Open minus Close = buckets processed)
  Units Amount = Daily revenue in MWK
  Late         = Rate per 20-litre bucket in MWK
  Ma units atha = "Tokens are finished" — node out of electricity

REMITTANCE CONFIRMATION:
  "Ndatumiza Ndalama" = "I have sent the money"
  "Ma Units Atha"     = "Tokens are finished / electricity exhausted"
  "Magets Kulibe"     = "Escom Outage / ESCOM Blackout" 
  "Ndachotsapo [X]"   = "I deducted [X]" — operator deductions before remitting

DEDUCTION CATEGORIES OBSERVED:
  Furez / Fulez    = Freezer or compressor component
  Maliselo         = Sieves / screens
  Gilizi           = Grease
  Malamba          = Belts
  Ma bauti         = Bolts / fasteners
  Owotchelela      = Welder / welding work
  Yamulewa / Amulewa = Casual/piece worker payment
  Yoyendela        = Transport / travel expenses
  Masefa           = Sieves (also ndachotsap for sefa purchase)
  Mano             = Grinding teeth / hammers (consumable)
